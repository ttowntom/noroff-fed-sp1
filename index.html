Where Wonder Meets knowledge

We believe science should not be confined to the textbook, but brought to live through exhibits. This is why we have over 1000 different exhibits on the many varied subjects of science to explore.

Explore our Exhibition Spaces

Welcome to the Community Science Museum!

Step into a realm where imagination knows no bounds and learning is an exhilarating adventure. Our museum is not just a place; it's a journey through the realms of evolution, the mysteries of the cosmos, the marvels of robotics, and the delicate balance of ecosystems.

Embrace the joy of discovery, the thrill of exploration, and the power of learning. Welcome, and let's embark on an incredible journey together!

Featured exhibitions

Cosmology

Cosmic Odyssey: Journey Through the Universe

Embark on an awe-inspiring adventure through space and time with 'Cosmic Odyssey.' Uncover the mysteries of the cosmos, from dazzling galaxies to mesmerizing nebulae. Explore the birth and evolution of stars, and get a front-row seat to the wonders of our universe. Through interactive exhibits and stunning visuals, this exhibition promises to ignite your curiosity about the vastness of space.

Visit the exhibition page

Evolution

Evolution Unearthed: Tracing Life's Remarkable Journey

Unveil the secrets of adaptation, survival, and transformation as you delve into the history of life forms. Encounter ancient fossils, engage with hands-on activities, and discover the fascinating processes that shaped life as we know it today.

Visit the exhibition page

Robotics and AI

RoboRealm: Where Robotics and AI Converge

Immerse yourself in the cutting-edge realm of robotics and artificial intelligence. Engage with interactive robots, witness the marvels of automation, and explore the ethical and creative dimensions of AI.

Visit the exhibition page

Ecology

EcoSphere: Navigating Nature's Web

Dive into the intricate tapestry of ecosystems that sustain life on Earth. Immerse yourself in interactive exhibits that highlight the interconnectedness of all living things, and discover how each small action can make a profound impact on the planet we call home.

Visit the exhibition page
